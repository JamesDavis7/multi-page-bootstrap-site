OUR SERVICES

What we provide and how we go about doing it

Based in Newcastle Upon Tyne, we at AutoPhoto specialise in photographing anything with four wheels to show their true character and spirit.

Our staff are able to provide a number of services, but our most popular include:

  • Providing a portfolio of images for insurance purposes
  • Providing a portfolio of images to assist in the sale of a car
  • Create framed artwork for home, office or garage walls or a high quality printed ‘coffee table’ book to showcase your car (further display options are available)

Whether you are looking for a small selection of images, an extensive portfolio or just want some high quality, professional standard photos of your pride and joy, our staff work discreetly without complication or fuss at either your home or an alternative suitable location (if a location fee is required this will be added to the price).

Following an initial conversation with one of our photographers to establish your requirements we"ll meet at the chosen location and spend between 2 and 4 hours (depending on your requirements) photographing your car from a variety of angles, including a selection of "detail" images that may be unique to your car. The agreed date and time for the shoot may have to be rearranged depending on the weather and possible covid restrictions.

Following the session, you will receive a CD containing a selection of fully edited, hi-resolution images for your own personal use via post. Alternatively, we can email you these upon request.

For further information regarding pricing, availability and to discuss your particular requirements please get in touch using our enquiry form located on the Contact Page.
